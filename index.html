Who am I?
Hi! I'm Issac Li, a current sophomore studying Computer Science at Princeton University. I grew up in Hong Kong, and have lived in the Bay Area ever since 2014. I am passionate about the role that AI can and will have in our world going forward. Over the past two years, I have been involved in a variety of positions that have exposed me to the use of AI. This past winter, I worked to develop an AI chatbot for a non-profit organization that was fine-tuned for its intended audience. Additionally, I have been collaborating with a research group on AI Alignment, and have developed effective scaleable oversight techniques. To find out more about my experiences, click here !
